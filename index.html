Program Overview

Welcome to Stellar Journey to Mastery: Monthly Builder Challenges.

The program is structured, progressive, and level-based to help developers build and ship real applications on Stellar.

Program Structure & Tracks

  • Builder Track (Belt Progression): $6,000 prize pool.
  • Open Source Track: $2,000 prize pool.

Belt System

  • Level 1 - White Belt: Build wallets and submit first on-chain transactions.
  • Level 2 - Yellow Belt: Multi-wallet flows, smart contracts, event handling.
  • Level 3 - Orange Belt: Build and test a complete mini dApp.
  • Level 4 - Green Belt: Advanced smart contracts and production readiness.
  • Level 5 - Blue Belt: Ship a real MVP and onboard first 5 users.
  • Level 6 - Black Belt: Scale to 20 users and present at Demo Day.

Who Can Join?

  • This challenge is open to everyone.
  • Curious about building on Stellar.
  • Basic frontend or full-stack development experience.
  • Want hands-on Web3 experience instead of only tutorials.
  • Enjoy learning by building real things.

What You Will Gain

  • Win from an $8,000 prize pool.
  • Earn belt badges as proof of progress.
  • Become eligible for grants and Demo Day opportunities.
  • Gain visibility in the Stellar ecosystem.
  • Build a portfolio of real, shipped applications.
